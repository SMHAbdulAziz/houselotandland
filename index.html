Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about selling your property

What types of properties do you buy?

We buy houses, lots, land, and distressed properties in any condition - whether they're vacant or occupied. No property is too damaged or too small for us to consider.

How quickly can I get a cash offer?

We provide no-obligation cash offers within 24 hours of receiving your property information. The process is fast and straightforward.

No! We buy properties as-is. You don't need to make any repairs, clean up, or spend money fixing anything. We'll take care of everything.
